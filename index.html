Training

It is important to train your body to go long distances, but you
don't want to overtrain and risk injury.

Nutrition

If you're training for an ultra-marathon, you're going to want
to eat a well balanced diet. Your diet should include whole grains,
lean proteins, healthy fats, and (obviously) fruits and vegetables.
